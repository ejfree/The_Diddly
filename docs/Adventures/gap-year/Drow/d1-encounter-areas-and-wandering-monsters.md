## D1 — Encounter Areas & Tunnel Wandering Monster Tables

Chapter 6 covers the general underworld and passage types. Chapter 7 covers the first fixed encounters on the Route to the Warrens. Wandering monster tables here apply to all tunnel travel by passage size. Chapter 8 (Grand Cavern areas 1–40) has its own wandering monster table — see [d1-ch8-grand-cavern.md](d1-ch8-grand-cavern.md).

## Encounter Areas — Chapter 7: The Route to the Warrens

| Area | Title |
|------|-------|
| encounter area d-3 | Drow Checkpoint (tunnel nexus south, primary passage) |
| encounter area m-12 | Illithid Spy Post (westernmost illithid outpost, pool cave) |

**Encounter Area D-3** — A drow checkpoint with two separate patrol groups: males to the left (southwest), females to the right. Males have two pickets on duty. Located south of the tunnel nexus, dimly lit by phosphorescent lichens. Uses encounter piece II.

**Encounter Area M-12** — Westernmost illithid spy post, known to the drow but currently ignored. Uses encounter piece II (reversed). Contains a cave pool, a bound drow merchant captive, 2 illithids, and 12 wererats. Cave lies in a secondary or tertiary passage.

## Footnotes

**a.** If within 6 miles of the Kuo-Toan shrine, replace with a Kuo-Toan cleric party.

**b.** If within 6 miles of the Vault of the Drow, replace with a drow patrol (mixed type).

**c.** If within 6 miles of the Kuo-Toan shrine or other Kuo-Toan outpost, replace with a Kuo-Toan war party.

**d.** If within 6 miles of the Kuo-Toan shrine, replace with a band of Kuo-Toan pilgrims.

**e.** If within 6 miles of the Vault of the Drow, replace with a Type I, II, or III demon.

## Drow Patrol & Merchant Composition (Quick Reference)

### Patrols

**Male or Female Patrol:**

- 7–10 Fighters
- 1 Patrol Leader
- 1 Patrol Commander

**Mixed Patrol:**

- 2 Male Fighters
- 1 Male Patrol Leader
- Variable non-drow troops (see table below)

### Non-Drow Patrol Troops

Roll 1d6, add +0 for Primary passage, +6 for Secondary, +12 for Tertiary.
