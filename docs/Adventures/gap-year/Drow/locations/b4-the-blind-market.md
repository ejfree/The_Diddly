# B4 — The Blind Market

## **The Blind Market**
A secondary grimlock camp operating at the edge of Elder Vrakt's authority — a splinter group of 15–20 younger grimlocks who have broken from the Eyeless Village's drow-accommodation policy and set up their own operation in a wide crack off the main passage. They are not at war with Vrakt's group, but they are not bound by the truce either.

### **Description**
A secondary grimlock camp in a wide crack off the main passage, operating at the edge of Elder Vrakt's authority. A splinter group of 15–20 younger grimlocks who have broken from the Eyeless Village's drow-accommodation policy. They are not at war with Vrakt's group, but they are not bound by the truce either. They do not speak to outsiders as a rule but will trade with anyone who finds them by sound (DC 14 Perception to hear the tapping signals they use for communication).

### **Features & Terrain**

- **Lighting**: Total darkness

- **Surfaces**: Wide crack off the main passage; tight squeeze at the entrance

- **Objects**: Stolen drow equipment — signal horns, torch-chalk, manacle keys, Undercommon-inscribed tablets; three Velouryn courier communications (unopened, wax-sealed)

- **Doors & Exits**: Crack entrance — tight squeeze (Medium creatures fit; anything larger requires a DC 13 Athletics check and makes noise)

- **Special Interactions**: Grimlocks communicate via tapping signals (DC 14 Perception to hear from outside); will hear any failed Athletics check at the entrance

### **Smells & Sounds**

- **Smells**:

- **Sounds**: Tapping signals used for internal communication; DC 14 Perception to detect from outside

### **Inhabitants**

- **Passive Creatures**: 15–20 younger grimlock traders

- **Hostile Threats**:

- **Unique NPCs**: Scratch — a grimlock who lost his primary echolocation in a rockfall and compensates through exceptionally fine touch-sensing. He deals in written material he cannot read himself, trading by weight and apparent value. He has three tablets in current stock that are Velouryn courier communications — unopened, wax-sealed. He has no idea what they contain.

### **Traps & Hazards**

- **Trap Name & Type**: Tight Entrance — noise hazard

- **Trigger**: Anything larger than Medium attempting to enter; failed DC 13 Athletics check

- **Effect**: Noise — grimlocks inside are alerted

- **Disarm**: DC 13 Athletics check to squeeze through silently (Medium creatures pass automatically)

### **Loot & Rewards**

- **Visible Loot**: Stolen drow equipment — signal horns, torch-chalk, manacle keys

- **Hidden Loot**: Small hoard of Undercommon-inscribed tablets taken from ambushed couriers

- **Magical Items**:

### **Additional Notes**
Drow Threat: Elder Vrakt does not officially know this market exists. A Dravun patrol sergeant does — she has been quietly purchasing recovered equipment here for six weeks, off-manifest, paying in raw iron. If the PCs are here when she arrives (1-in-8 chance per hour), the situation becomes complicated.

The three Velouryn courier tablets held by Scratch are unopened and wax-sealed — their contents are unknown to him.
