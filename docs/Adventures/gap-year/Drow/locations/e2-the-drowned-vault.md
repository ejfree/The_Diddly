# E2 — The Drowned Vault

## **The Drowned Vault**
Not the open crossing — a collapsed cavern system accessible from the eastern shore of the Sunless Sea, below the waterline, where a partial air pocket has persisted for centuries inside a partially flooded deep gnome vault. The entry is a submerged arch visible only to anyone who dives in the shallows near a distinctive double-spire rock formation at E1's eastern edge.

### **Description**
A collapsed cavern system accessible from the eastern shore of the Sunless Sea, below the waterline. A partial air pocket has persisted for centuries inside a partially flooded deep gnome vault. Three chambers: a flooded antechamber, a partially-air chamber with a collapsed ceiling letting in a trickle of brine, and a dry inner vault sealed for at least 200 years. The svirfneblin stonework is unmistakably old: carved resonance columns, a stone table with an intact inscription in deep gnome script.

### **Features & Terrain**

- **Lighting**: No natural light; requires a light source to navigate safely

- **Surfaces**: Flooded antechamber; partially-flooded second chamber; dry inner vault with svirfneblin stonework

- **Objects**: Carved resonance columns; stone table with intact inscription in deep gnome script; sealed chest (trapped); second sealed door in the far wall of the dry vault fitted with resonance-column technology

- **Doors & Exits**: Submerged arch entry (40-foot swim); second sealed door in the dry vault's far wall — leads somewhere below, has not been opened

- **Special Interactions**: DC 16 Perception to spot the submerged arch from above water; the swim through is 40 feet, manageable for a prepared party but lethal without a light source or water breathing

### **Smells & Sounds**

- **Smells**: Brine, old stone, cold salt water

- **Sounds**: Trickle of brine through collapsed ceiling in the second chamber; silence in the dry vault

- **Hostile Threats**: A giant blind albino crayfish (CR 5) has nested in the flooded antechamber. It attacks anything that disturbs the silt. It is very large for the space and fighting it in water is difficult.

### **Additional Notes**
The Inscription: A transit record listing convoys that passed through this checkpoint before the vault flooded. One entry, dated in the old gnomish calendar, lists a cargo simply as *"the three voices — do not open below the third level."* No further explanation.

Connection: The dry vault has a second sealed door in the far wall — stone, fitted with the same resonance-column technology used in the Ironbell Warren. It leads somewhere below. It has not been opened. (GM note: this is the "path below" from Fragment Three of the speaking-stone prophecies at Blingdenstone.)
