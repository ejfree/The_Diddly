## D1 Chapter 8 — Warrens of the Troglodytes

**Encounter area q-18/19** — The Grand Cavern. A massive underground complex 200–300 feet wide, 200 feet long, 175–250 feet high. Ceiling drips with stalactites, floor with stalagmites, broken stone throughout. Multiple factions (drow, bugbears, troglodytes, trolls) coexist under drow authority.

Wandering monster check: **1 in 12 per turn.** Roll 1d4 on the appropriate half-table below (western or eastern side of the cavern).

### Area 2 — South Gallery

Mineral deposits from dripping water fill this passage. Home to **14 piercers** near the middle — 1–3 drop on each creature passing beneath. A Perception DC 18 check detects irregular shadows above before entering the drop zone. No treasure.

Piercers are stalactite-like cave predators; immobile until they drop; each can attack once per encounter before retreating slowly up the cave wall.

### Area 7 — Sunken Cave (Lich Lair)

Two narrow stair-like terraced flights descend ~20 feet into a low cave that **appears totally dark and deserted** — permanent *illusory scene* (DC 38 Will disbelieve). Lair of **Asberdies the Lich** (legendary wizard, Lvl 20). Will not bother passers-by; attacks only if entered.

**Strategy:** Prefers *ventriloquism* deception, *repulsion*, *reverse gravity*, and *wish* variants to deter; uses *summon monster* and summoned air elementals to avoid personal melee. Prefers *dimension door* or *teleport* while *invisible* to flee rather than fight to the death. Will erect *wall of force* and use *time stop* to strip spell components and holy symbols from paralyzed enemies.

### Area 40 — Magic Pool

A broad, clear pool (~8 feet deep at center), fed by an underground stream. Cavern inhabitants drink from it regularly. **Drinking has no effect.** The pool does not communicate. The pool radiates a faint magical aura (DC 15 Arcana or Nature to identify); gems inside it do not radiate.

**At the center (under a light sand layer — Perception DC 16 to notice without diving):** 89 gems (10 gp each).

**Gem mechanics:**

- If any gems are removed from the pool for more than 10 minutes → they turn a like number of gems within 30 feet into worthless quartz (1 gp each).
- If a character tosses 1–4 gems *into* the pool → a like number of gems *in their possession* improve by one quality category (flaws cleared; 10 gp → 50 gp, or 50 gp → 100 gp).
- Drow merchants who know the secret always toss only 10 gp gems.
