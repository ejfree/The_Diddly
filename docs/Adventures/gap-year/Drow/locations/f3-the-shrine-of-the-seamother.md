# F3 — The Shrine of the Sea Mother

## **The Shrine of the Sea Mother**
The sacred heart of the kuo-toa complex. A vast flooded cathedral — 10 feet of brine on the floor, illuminated by enormous bioluminescent corals. The Blibdoolpoolp idol (15 feet tall, carved obsidian, lobster claws) dominates the far end. High Servant Kloarek holds court from a raised dais surrounded by kuo-toa monks and armed whips.
