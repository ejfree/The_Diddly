# B5 — The Cold Seep

## **The Cold Seep**
A section of passage where a sub-zero underground current vents through fissures in the floor, dropping the ambient temperature sharply and coating the stone in a thin layer of condensation that becomes near-invisible ice in the cold zone. The cold zone extends roughly 80 feet along the passage.

### **Description**
A section of passage where a sub-zero underground current vents through fissures in the floor, dropping the ambient temperature sharply and coating the stone in a thin layer of condensation that becomes near-invisible ice in the cold zone. The cold zone extends roughly 80 feet along the passage.

### **Features & Terrain**

- **Lighting**:

- **Surfaces**: Stone coated in near-invisible ice; fissures in the floor (2 feet wide — too narrow to fall into)

- **Objects**: Three frozen grimlock bodies visible in the fissures, partially preserved. One clutches a wrapped bundle — a grimlock-made map.

- **Doors & Exits**: 80-foot cold zone along the passage

- **Special Interactions**: Sub-zero temperature affects perception; cloaker benefits from cold-slowed prey

### **Smells & Sounds**

- **Smells**: Cold, mineral air

- **Sounds**: Venting air from fissures

### **Inhabitants**

- **Passive Creatures**:

- **Hostile Threats**: A cloaker has taken up residence against the ceiling of the cold zone. It has been feeding on grimlocks and has learned caution — it will not attack a group of four or more.

- **Unique NPCs**:

### **Traps & Hazards**

- **Trap Name & Type**: Near-Invisible Ice — movement hazard

- **Trigger**: Crossing the ice at speed

- **Effect**: DC 15 Acrobatics or fall prone and slide 1d6 × 5 feet toward the nearest fissure; sliding into a fissure deals 1d6 cold damage and restrains the creature until pulled free

- **Disarm**: Move carefully (no speed penalty trigger)

### **Loot & Rewards**

- **Visible Loot**: Three frozen grimlock bodies in the fissures

- **Hidden Loot**: Wrapped bundle clutched by one frozen grimlock — a grimlock-made map, crude but accurate, showing the passage layout between B3 and C1 with four locations marked by a scratched symbol that appears to mean "food here." Two of the four locations correspond to known encounter sites. Two do not.

- **Magical Items**:

### **Additional Notes**
The cloaker benefits from the temperature dropping prey's perception and has learned that cold-slowed creatures are easier to manage. It has become cautious through experience — groups of four or more are left alone.
