# A8 — The Fungal Shelf

## **The Fungal Shelf**
A naturally terraced section of tunnel where a step-change in geology has created three broad stone shelves, each about waist height, running along the left wall for 60 feet. The shelves are carpeted in dense fungal growth — zurkhwood caps, glowing puffballs, and several clusters of edible cave mushrooms. The air here is noticeably different: warmer, more humid, faintly sweet.
