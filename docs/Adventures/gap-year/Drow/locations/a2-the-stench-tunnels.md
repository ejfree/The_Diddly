# A2 — The Stench Tunnels

## **The Stench Tunnels**
The main troglodyte warrens — a series of low-ceilinged passages thick with musk, rot, and the smell of raw meat. The tribe here is substantial: 40–60 adults plus young. They treat the drow as gods and gods as things to be feared. They will not willingly aid travelers unless intimidated or bribed with surface flesh.
