# H2 — The Chain Bridge

## **The Chain Bridge**
A natural chasm, 40 feet across and 80 feet deep, splits the interior of the main cavern cluster between Ironhold and Ashhold. The only crossing is a massive iron-chain-and-plank bridge, 12 feet wide, maintained jointly by both clans. Joint maintenance means each clan blames the other for every repair that doesn't happen.

Currently: three planks are structurally unsound (both clans know; neither will pay for replacement because that would imply responsibility). A loaded ore wagon crossed three days ago and the bridge held, but the vibration loosened a chain anchor on the Ashhold side. Clan Greldak has not told Clan Hrunkar. Clan Hrunkar has not inspected the bridge since the wagon crossing. The PCs crossing with a full load at a run would require an Athletics or Acrobatics check. The PCs crossing in a fight, or while someone above drops weight on it, would require significantly more.

### **Description**
A massive iron-chain-and-plank bridge, 12 feet wide, spanning a 40-foot chasm 80 feet deep. The bridge connects Ironhold and Ashhold across the natural fissure that splits their cavern cluster. It is the only crossing.

### **Features & Terrain**

- **Lighting**: Whatever ambient light the surrounding cavern provides — no dedicated lighting on the bridge itself.

- **Surfaces**: Iron chain rails, wooden planks. Three planks are currently structurally unsound. One chain anchor on the Ashhold side was loosened by a recent ore wagon crossing.

- **Objects**: The bridge itself. Iron mooring rings on both sides. Chain tensioners.

- **Doors & Exits**: Ironhold on one side, Ashhold on the other. No alternative crossing exists.

- **Special Interactions**: Crossing at a run with a full load requires Athletics or Acrobatics check. Crossing during a fight, or with additional weight dropped onto the bridge, requires significantly higher checks. The loosened Ashhold anchor is unknown to Clan Hrunkar — failure under stress could be catastrophic.

### **Smells & Sounds**

- **Smells**: Iron, wood, the dry mineral smell of the chasm.

- **Sounds**: Chain creak under load. Wind from the chasm below. The three unsound planks flex noticeably underfoot.

### **Additional Notes**
The political dimension: Clan Greldak (Ashhold) knows about the loosened anchor and has not told Clan Hrunkar (Ironhold). Clan Hrunkar has not inspected the bridge since the ore wagon crossing. The deferred maintenance is a structural metaphor for the clan relationship — each side is waiting for the other to blink first on responsibility. When the bridge eventually fails, both clans will blame the other and both will be partly right.
