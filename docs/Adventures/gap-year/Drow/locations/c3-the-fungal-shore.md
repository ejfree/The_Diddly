# C3 — The Fungal Shore

## **The Fungal Shore**
The riverbank widens into a massive fungal forest — towering zurkhwood columns, glowing shriekers, violet fungus clusters. Beautiful in an alien way. The fungi provide dim phosphorescent light, the first natural illumination since Sector A. Spore clouds are a hazard; sound triggers shriekers which alert everything within 500 feet.

### **Description**
The riverbank widens into a massive fungal forest — towering zurkhwood columns, glowing shriekers, violet fungus clusters. Beautiful in an alien way. The fungi provide dim phosphorescent light, the first natural illumination since Sector A. Spore clouds are a hazard; sound triggers shriekers which alert everything within 500 feet.

### **Features & Terrain**

- **Lighting**: Dim phosphorescent light from the fungal growth — the first natural illumination since Sector A

- **Surfaces**: Riverbank cavern; dense fungal growth throughout including zurkhwood columns

- **Objects**: Zurkhwood columns (can be fashioned into a raft or simple weapons); edible dried fungi (basic rations, 3 days' worth accessible with a Nature check)

- **Doors & Exits**:

- **Special Interactions**: Sound triggers shriekers, alerting everything within 500 feet; spore clouds are a passive hazard

### **Smells & Sounds**

- **Smells**: Fungal spores, damp stone, riverbank

- **Sounds**: Shriekers will wail if sound triggers them; bioluminescent glow is near-silent

### **Inhabitants**

- **Passive Creatures**: 2d4 violet fungus clusters in the deeper grove; shriekers at high density throughout

- **Hostile Threats**: A hunting party of 4 quaggoths (House Dravun) is harvesting zurkhwood here. They carry a carved Dravun token and will not attack if shown a drow insignia. Otherwise extremely aggressive.

- **Unique NPCs**:

### **Traps & Hazards**

- **Trap Name & Type**: Shriekers — sound alarm hazard

- **Trigger**: Any loud sound in the fungal shore area

- **Effect**: Shriekers wail, alerting everything within 500 feet

- **Disarm**: Move silently; avoid disturbing the fungus

### **Loot & Rewards**

- **Visible Loot**: Zurkhwood columns (craftable into raft or simple weapons)

- **Hidden Loot**: Edible dried fungi — basic rations, 3 days' worth accessible with a Nature check

- **Magical Items**:

### **Additional Notes**
The quaggoth hunting party carries a carved Dravun token. They respond to drow insignia with deference but are extremely aggressive toward anyone who appears unaffiliated.
