# The Ironspine — Forward Garrison

## **The Ironspine**
The Ironspine is Grimstone Holds' forward military installation — a separate fortified outpost carved into a natural rock buttress that narrows the West Passage to a single-wagon choke point. Travelers approaching from the direction of Xil encounter the Ironspine before they see the main city. It is not subtle about its purpose.

### **Description**
A fortified outpost carved into a natural rock buttress at the West Passage chokepoint. Three successive iron gates across the passage, each with a portcullis controlled from above. Barracks carved into the rock wall on both sides. A signal tower at the top of the buttress, visible from both the Holds and a mile down the passage. The garrison lives inside the chokepoint.

### **Features & Terrain**

- **Lighting**: Torches at each gate position and the signal tower. The approach is lit enough that anything moving toward the gate is visible from 200 feet.

- **Surfaces**: Cut stone throughout. Iron gate frames set directly into the rock. The portcullis mechanisms are above, accessed through ladder shafts invisible from below.

- **Objects**: Three iron portcullis gates. Signal tower with a fire-beacon and mirror. Small armory: crossbows, bolts, and enough oil to make the passage impassable for a week. Inspection tables at Gate One.

- **Doors & Exits**: The West Passage — the only route. Ironspine is the only gate. There is no passage around it.

- **Special Interactions**: Papers checked at Gate One. Cargo inspected at Gate Two. Everything unusual logged. The logs are Drevrak's and go only to the Overking.

### **Smells & Sounds**

- **Smells**: Iron, oil, torch smoke. The armory: sharp smell of crossbow oil and dry wood.

- **Sounds**: Gate machinery when portcullises move. Guards' footsteps on the stone walkways above. The signal tower: wind.

### **Traps & Hazards**

- **Trap Name & Type**: Three-gate portcullis system — defensive

- **Trigger**: Gate-Captain's order, or automated drop via release lever

- **Effect**: Each portcullis seals independently. With all three down, the passage is completely blocked. Oil from the armory can be deployed through ceiling ports at each gate position.

- **Disarm**: Portcullis controls are above, in shafts accessible only from inside the garrison.

### **Additional Notes**
**Role in Play:** Every significant traveler entering or leaving Grimstone Holds passes through Ironspine. Papers are checked, cargo is inspected, and everything unusual is logged. Drevrak's logs are one of the most complete records of Underdark traffic in the region. Clan Solvrak has been trying to get access to those logs for years; Drevrak reports only to the Overking.

**Population:** ~400 duergar (rotating garrison duty from all six clans), 120 slave laborers.
