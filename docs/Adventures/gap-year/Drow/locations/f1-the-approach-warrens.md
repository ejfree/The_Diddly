# F1 — The Approach Warrens

## **The Approach Warrens**
The outer kuo-toa village — a crude but densely populated cavern complex of mud-and-stone structures, fish markets, slave pens (mostly grimlocks and captured surface folk), and shrines to Blibdoolpoolp on every corner. The population here is roughly sane, just alien. Trade is possible.
