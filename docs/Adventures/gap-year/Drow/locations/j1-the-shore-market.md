# J1 — The Shore Market

## **The Shore Market**
A quarter-mile of Sunless Sea shoreline at the Warrens' northern edge, grown into a semi-regulated district distinct from the Exchange in the city's interior. Where the Exchange is customs-controlled and indoor, the Shore Market is open, chaotic, and governed primarily by whoever is physically present. Water-borne goods arrive here first. Kuo-toa fish-traders beach their boats here. Mindspire trade vessels anchor off the dock and send indoctrinated servants ashore with manifests.

### **Description**
A quarter-mile of open shoreline. Three wooden docks extending into the Sunless Sea. A permanent open-air fish market with fixed stalls. Warehouse row on the shore — bulk water-borne cargo staged here before moving to the Exchange. A stone hut at the eastern end: the harbormaster's post.

### **Features & Terrain**

- **Lighting**: Bioluminescent organisms in the Sunless Sea provide ambient illumination over the water. The shore itself is lit by vendor fires and lanterns — bright by Underdark standards, chaotic and inconsistent.

- **Surfaces**: Wet stone and packed earth along the shore. Wooden dock planking — maintained at significant expense, always in some state of repair from the Sea's fluctuating water levels.

- **Objects**: Three wooden docks. Fish market stalls (permanent). Shore warehouses. Harbormaster's stone hut. Dockmaster Vreth's log (inside the hut — technically Warden Crael's property). Dock fee collection box.

- **Doors & Exits**: Shore connects to Warrens city streets to the south. The Sunless Sea to the north. No formal gate — the Shore Market has no customs inspection point.

- **Special Interactions**: Arriving by boat draws less scrutiny than arriving through the southern tunnel. Vreth's log contains records of every water-borne arrival — including the Mindspire sealed cargo deliveries. Copies have been sold to Clan Solvrak (Grimstone Holds) for six months.

### **Smells & Sounds**

- **Smells**: Fish, salt (faintly — the Sunless Sea is not fully saline), wet wood, smoke from vendor fires.

- **Sounds**: Market noise — vendor calls, water lapping at the docks, raft and boat movement. Derro dock workers. The rhythmic creak of the docks under load. On Mindspire vessel days: unusual quiet around the anchorage point.

### **Inhabitants**

- **Passive Creatures**: Kuo-toa fish-traders (variable number, dependent on boat traffic).

### **Additional Notes**
**The Mindspire Presence:** On the third and seventh day of each tenday, a Mindspire vessel arrives with sealed cargo — Khyber shard fragments purchased from the Seam-Keepers in a transaction that bypasses the Exchange entirely and goes through Vreth's dock fees. Warden Crael knows about this arrangement. The Pale Choir acolytes know the Elder Brain is buying shards and have strong opinions about it they have not expressed publicly yet.

**Role in Play:** The Shore Market is the easiest entry point into the Warrens by water — arriving by boat draws less scrutiny than coming through the southern tunnel with no visible cargo. It is also the place to arrange passage back across the Sunless Sea, find a kuo-toa who will take passengers, or intercept Mindspire communications before they reach the main city.

**Population:** 200–400 at any given time (highly variable with boat traffic).
