## **The Veil of Shadows**

### **Description**
The last natural cavern before the city gates. The tunnel ceiling rises to 80 feet and the walls fall away — the PCs emerge onto a promontory overlooking an enormous underground void. Across the void, barely visible through haze and fungal spore-cloud: the lights of Xilthraen. Priestesses of Lolth bless passing soldiers from a shrine carved into the promontory wall. War banners hang from ceiling anchors. The sound of the city — drums, distant shouting, the smell of cookfires — drifts across.

### **Features & Terrain**

- **Lighting**: Distant lights of Xilthraen visible through haze and spore-cloud; shrine torch-light on the promontory

- **Surfaces**: Promontory overlooking an enormous underground void; 20-foot-wide rope-and-stone bridge spanning the void

- **Objects**: Shrine to Lolth carved into the promontory wall; war banners hanging from ceiling anchors

- **Doors & Exits**: 20-foot-wide rope-and-stone bridge to the Gate of Silken Chains (entrance to Xilthraen proper); bridge-wardens check tokens at the crossing

- **Special Interactions**: Two drow bridge-wardens check tokens at the bridge; Priestesses of Lolth bless passing soldiers at the shrine

### **Smells & Sounds**

- **Smells**: Cookfires drifting from the city across the void; fungal spore-cloud

- **Sounds**: Drums and distant shouting from Xilthraen; priestesses at the shrine; the void itself
