# A7 — The Split Passage

## **The Split Passage**
The tunnel forks into three branches for the first time since the Shattered Gate. Two of the branches rejoin within 200 feet and lead toward Sector B. The third descends sharply and does not rejoin — it goes somewhere else entirely.

### **Description**
The tunnel forks into three branches for the first time since the Shattered Gate. Two of the branches rejoin within 200 feet and lead toward Sector B. The third descends sharply and does not rejoin — it goes somewhere else entirely. No markings. The troglodytes know to take the left or center fork and avoid the right. They will not say why if asked.
