## **The Echoing Chasm**
A long natural chasm with a 60-foot drop to unseen water below. The only crossing is a natural stone arch, 4 feet wide. The acoustics are extraordinary — a whisper at one end is audible as a murmur at the other. Grimlocks use it as an ambush point.
