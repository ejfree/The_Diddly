# Lisa Siviridion — Level 15 Emerald Dragon Fighter
**"The battlefield is a formula. I have solved it."**

---

## ✦ Character Concept

Lisa is a young Emerald Dragon who chose to learn swordsmanship the way her kind approach everything: obsessively, analytically, and with extensive modification of the tools involved. Her twin katanas are custom-engineered masterworks she calls **Theorem** and **Proof** — each blade fitted with precision-machined housings that allow volatile alchemical charges to be detonated on contact. She fights with mathematical precision, her every movement calculated, her explosions controlled variables.

**Personality:** Dry wit, relentlessly curious, mildly condescending to anyone who doesn't understand what she's doing (which is almost everyone). Keeps a leather-bound notebook of combat observations. Has strong opinions about metallurgy.

## ✦ Combat Gameplan

**Round 1:**
1. Enter **Graceful Poise** stance (free if you have Stance Savant, otherwise 1 action)
2. **Double Slice** — two attacks at no extra MAP penalty (agile off-hand)
3. **Two-Weapon Flurry** (Press) — two more strikes in one action if needed

**When surrounded:**
- **Explode** (Inventor Unstable action) — burst of fire around her, then DC 15 flat check; her Legendary Crafting brings this to DC 13
- Follow with **Attack of Opportunity** reactions on anyone moving through her reach

**Against dangerous single targets:**
- Twin Parry for the AC bonus
- Twin Riposte on critical misses against her

**Sonic breath weapon** (Emerald Dragon): Save for crowds or when she needs area denial without spending the Explode action.

## ✦ Key Mechanics Summary

- **Dual katanas** with Deadly d8 — crits roll extra damage dice
- **Agile Grace + Graceful Poise** = reduced MAP on all attacks in stance
- **Two-Weapon Flurry** = two strikes for one action (Press)
- **Explode** = AoE burst, unstable but powerful (DC 13 flat check at Legendary Crafting)
- **Legendary Crafting** = can craft and modify her own weapons, gadgets, and innovations
- **Sonic resistance** (Emerald trait) = relevant defensive DR
- **Psionic breath weapon** = cone of sonic damage, basic Reflex, scales with level
