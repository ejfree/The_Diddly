# Symphonic Coliseum

**Map #31 | Water Quad (South)**

AKA "SyCo" (Psycho). Concert hall for the Io'lokar Philharmonic as well as the university symphony and many other groups. One of the premier performance locations in the universe. Done in a wildly avant-garde style with an over-the-top dragon theme throughout.
