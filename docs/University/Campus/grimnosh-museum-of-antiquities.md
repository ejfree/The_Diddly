# Grimnosh Museum of Antiquities

**Map #32 | Water Quad (South)**

Similar in character to the Hermitage or the Louvre. Houses many magical artifacts from eras long past along with art exhibits spanning all styles. Artifacts somewhat litter the place in some sections. The sub-basement floors hold thousands of exhibits — none of real value. Truly valuable pieces are on loan from private hordes and embedded with trackers. Sits on the same island as the Grimnosh Annex (#33), with shared sub-basements.
