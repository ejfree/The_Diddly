# Mistwood Shoals

**Water Quad Housing | Water Quad (South)**

Built into and around a tree originating from the earth zone that has grown mangrove-like into the water quad. Structures look woven from giant reeds and seaweed — strong but flexible. A permanent magical mist drifts through the settlement, and temperature and wind levels can be controlled as desired.
