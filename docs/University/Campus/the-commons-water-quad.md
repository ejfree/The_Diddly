# The Commons - Water Quad

Map Location: #30, Water Quad - South (near campus center)

The central student hub of The Diddly. The Commons serves as the student lounge and main cafeteria for Eggies (freshmen) and Drakes (sophomores), with smaller dedicated dining rooms for Wyverns and Primus. A special faculty dining facility is also on site. This is also where all campus mail is received and distributed. A relaxing beach area is attached to the facility along the water's edge.
