# Rewot Terra

Map Location: #14, Earth Quad - West

A massive hole in the ground — an inverted silo or downward tower — sitting between the center of the Earth Quad and the Grand Ol' Diddly. It descends to the center of the earth itself, with evil things lurking in the deep abyss below. Students are strictly prohibited from exploring past Level 172, which also happens to be the portal to the Plane of Earth. Campus police enforce compliance with access restrictions.

The Main Administration Building (#16) occupies the western face of the hole, integrated directly into the pit wall.
