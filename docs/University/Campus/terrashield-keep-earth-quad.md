# Terrashield Keep

**Earth Quad Housing | Earth Quad (West)**

An actual castle — a war prize won by some dragon thousands of years ago. Fortified style with squat, powerful structures built from natural stone, wood, and earth elements throughout. The outer bastion walls house Drake apartments and common areas. The inner keep is reserved for Wyverns, with Primus students occupying the towers.
