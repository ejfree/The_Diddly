# Emberfall Heights

**Fire Quad Housing | Fire Quad (North)**

Towers built into terraced lava flows along a cliff face. One of the lavafalls cascading down the cliff is real and is prized by red dragons; the others are convincing illusions. Constant warm breezes move through open archways. Common spaces feature ember-stone hearths alongside outdoor terrace living areas clinging to the cliff face.
