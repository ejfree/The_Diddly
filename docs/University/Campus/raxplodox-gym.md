# Raxplodox Gym

**Map #1 | Fire Quad (North) / Earth Quad juncture**

AKA "The Rax." One of the foremost workout facilities in the universe. Stretching machines, training weights of all types, indoor courts, and whatever you need. The gymnasium building itself sits on the fire side exclusively — it was a donation from a Red Dragon Weyr. Connecting sports courts and fields extend into the earth quad.
